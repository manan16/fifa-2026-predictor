How the desk thinks

The model

A transparent pipeline you can argue with — every step is a deliberate, inspectable choice, not a black box.

01 · Inputs

Ratings

Live Elo blended with FIFA ranking for each nation.

02 · Strength

The gap

One strength number per side; the difference drives everything.

03 · Goals

Supremacy

The gap becomes expected-goal supremacy over a stage-aware total.

04 · Grid

Poisson + DC

A score grid with a Dixon-Coles correction for low-scoring draws.

05 · Output

Probabilities

Win, draw, advance — then optionally blended toward the market.
